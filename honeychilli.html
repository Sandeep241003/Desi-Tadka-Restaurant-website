Honey Chilli Potato Recipe

Introduction

Honey Chilli Potato is an irresistible Indo-Chinese dish known for its perfect balance of sweet and spicy flavors. Crispy fried potato wedges are tossed in a delectable honey and chilli sauce, creating a crunchy and flavorful appetizer that's loved by all.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  • 2-3 green chillies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup spring onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup capsicum, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds and spring onion greens for garnish

Instructions

  1. Combine all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Coat potato wedges in this mixture and deep fry until golden brown.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© garlic, ginger, and green chillies. Add spring onions and capsicum.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, ketchup, red chilli sauce, honey, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add fried potatoes, toss until well coated, and garnish with sesame seeds and spring onion greens.
