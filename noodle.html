Hakka Noodle Recipe

Introduction

Hakka Noodles is a popular Indo-Chinese dish known for its quick preparation and delightful flavors. These stir-fried noodles are loaded with colorful vegetables and seasoned with a savory blend of sauces, making it a favorite among noodle enthusiasts.

Ingredients

  • 200g Hakka noodles
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.), julienned
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook Hakka noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan, add minced garlic and grated ginger. Saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add julienned vegetables and cook until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
  4. Toss in cooked noodles, soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry until well combined.
  5. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
