Shahi Paneer Recipe

Introduction

Shahi Paneer, meaning "Royal Cottage Cheese," is a luxurious Indian dish renowned for its
creamy texture and rich flavor. Prepared with paneer in a cashew and cream-based gravy,
it features aromatic spices like cardamom and cinnamon. Ideal for festive occasions, this
regal delicacy is a beloved vegetarian classic in Indian cuisine, best enjoyed with naan
or fragrant basmati rice.
