Veg Biryani Recipe

Introduction

Vegetable Biryani is an aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, mix veggies, herbs & biryani spices.
This easy Veg Biryani is a total crowd pleaser! You will love this for everything
from busy night meals to holiday family dinners.




Ingredients:

  • 1 cup aged basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced onion
  • 1 green chili, slit or chopped (skip for kids)
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped beans
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped cauliflower

Instructions:

  1. Rinse 1 cup of basmati rice until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the rice in enough water for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp ghee in a pot.
  4. Add 1 bay leaf, 2 green cardamoms, 2 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick, and 1 star anise.
  5. Fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add 1/2 cup thinly sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  7. Add 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and fry until the raw smell goes away.
  8. Add 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes and fry until they turn soft and mushy.
  9. Add 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (carrots, beans, green peas, cauliflower, and potatoes) and fry for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Add 1/2 tsp red chili powder, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder,
    1/2 tsp garam masala powder, 1/2 tsp biryani masala powder, and salt as needed.Mix well.
  11. Add 1/2 cup yogurt and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  12. Add 1.5 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
  13. Drain the water from the soaked rice and add it to the boiling water. Mix well.
  14. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked.
  15. In a small bowl, soak 1/4 tsp saffron strands in 1 tbsp warm milk.
  16. Once the rice is cooked, turn off the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  17. Open the lid and sprinkle the saffron milk on top of the rice.
  18. Garnish with 2 tbsp chopped mint leaves and 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves.
  19. Serve hot with raita or any curry of your choice.
