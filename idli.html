Idli Recipe

Introduction

Idli is a popular South Indian steamed cake made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. These soft and fluffy delights are a staple breakfast item, often served with coconut chutney and sambar, creating a wholesome and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup idli rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter, adding water as needed. Add salt and let it ferment overnight.
  3. Grease idli molds and pour the batter into each mold. Steam for 10-12 minutes until the idlis are cooked through.
  4. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
