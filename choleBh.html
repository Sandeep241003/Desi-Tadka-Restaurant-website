Chole Bhature Recipe

Introduction

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish that consists of spicy chickpeas
(chole) served with deep-fried
bread (bhature). It's a delicious and hearty meal that is often
enjoyed for breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil soaked chickpeas with salt until tender.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until onions are golden.
  3. Add tomato puree, spices, and cook until oil separates.
  4. Stir in yogurt and boiled chickpeas. Simmer until the gravy thickens.
  5. For bhature, mix flour, semolina, yogurt, baking soda, and salt. Knead into a soft dough.
  6. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out into discs.
  7. Deep fry the bhature until they puff up and turn golden brown.
  8. Serve hot chole with bhature, garnished with chopped onions and coriander.
