DESI TADKA | DELHI


The first DESI TADKA opened its door in Delhi at Lodhi Road in 2013. This was Delhi’s bustling mall with grandeur Cinema screens by Sathyam and never have seen before in Delhi top lifestyle brands.

DESI TADKA is well known in the Delhi market and has been frequented by Celebrities over the years for good food and excellent service.

The menu is a fair share of North and South Indian keeping in mind the local palate.

DESI TADKA | BENGALURU


The Second SALT and the first in Bengaluru at the newest Lifestyle center located in the new social and Lifestyle hub of Bangalore, Whitefield.

It is situated on the third floor and has a balcony seating which overlooks its surroundings is best enjoyed in the evening.

This is one of the favorite Indian restaurants for corporates like HP, EY to name a few and the Saturday Brunch is a big hit amongst shoppers and families alike.

DESI TADKA | MUMBAI


The third SALT finds its home in Nexus Mall, Mumbai and busiest mall centrally located in the city with its popular retail & cinemas.

It has a view towards the bustling environment and hanging garden along with funny quotes adorning the walls.

With one of the highest footfalls in the city, Nexus Mall will act as a catalyst to make DESI TADKA one of the most favorite dine-in places for the people of Mumbai

DESI TADKA | LUCKNOW


Introducing a flavorful journey to indulge your senses! DESI TADKA Indian Restaurant’s latest outlet at Bhartiya Mall, Lucknow, promises an unforgettable culinary experience.

Immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of Indian flavors meticulously crafted by our expert chefs. From aromatic biryanis to delectable curries, our menu is a symphony of tastes that will transport you to the heart of India.

Join us at DESI TADKA and savor every bite of tradition and innovation, only at Bhartiya Mall. Your next memorable dining affair awaits!
