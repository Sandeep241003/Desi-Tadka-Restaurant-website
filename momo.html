Veg Momos Recipe

Introduction

Momos are delightful dumplings that originated in Tibet but have become immensely popular across various regions. These steamed or fried dumplings are filled with a flavorful mixture, making them a beloved street food and a favorite snack for many.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Water, as needed
  • 1 cup minced chicken or vegetables
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the momo dough by mixing all-purpose flour with water until it forms a smooth, elastic dough. Rest the dough for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger in oil. Add minced chicken or vegetables, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Cook until the filling is cooked through.
  3. Roll the dough into small circles, place a spoonful of filling in the center, and shape the momos as desired.
  4. Steam the momos for about 15-20 minutes or until the dough is cooked through. Serve hot, garnished with chopped green onions.
